About this site

In an everlasting quest to make information more accessible, we created this site to guide users through space. On the different pages you can view objects like planets, their position and more.

This map shows most of the observatories located on earth. You can search by name, location or just browse the map for observatories. Click on an observatory to view more information and statistics about it.
